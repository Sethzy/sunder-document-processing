# Product

## Users

Claims reviewers, legal operations teams, and document-heavy professional
services teams who need to process messy legal claim files under time pressure.
They work inside a task UI, scanning tables, opening source documents, checking
extracted fields, and producing report artifacts.

## Product Purpose

Sunder turns scattered PDFs, scans, photos, and combined legal claim documents
into a reviewable dossier. It classifies and splits files, extracts structured
data with citations, lets reviewers verify values against source pages, and
generates submission-ready reports.

Success means a reviewer can trust the workflow quickly without needing a
marketing explanation.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, professional.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic AI SaaS landing page, WhatsApp automation tool, CRM
suite, voice-cloning assistant, or decorative productivity dashboard. Avoid fake
demo surfaces, purple-blue gradients, oversized hero marketing, glassy cards,
bouncy motion, and features that imply capabilities not backed by the real
workflow.

## Design Principles

1. Product first: default to the authenticated workspace and real document
   workflow.
2. Evidence over theater: every polished surface should help users inspect
   files, citations, statuses, or generated artifacts.
3. Restrained confidence: use familiar product UI patterns, dense but readable
   layouts, and semantic state indicators.
4. Preserve trust: avoid fake data paths and avoid public copy that overstates
   what the app currently does.
5. Polish the happy path: make upload, review, extraction, and reports feel
   coherent before expanding scope.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, keyboard navigation, focus states, and form labels.
Respect reduced motion. Keep dense tables readable at desktop sizes and avoid
horizontal page overflow on mobile.
